Our Founder

Akbota is the inspiration and creator of MathMagic. Her outstanding achievements in mathematics and passion for teaching led to the creation of this course. Here are some of her impressive accomplishments:

These early successes and positive experiences became the foundation for creating MathMagic — a course that aims to inspire and support students on their path to mathematical mastery.
